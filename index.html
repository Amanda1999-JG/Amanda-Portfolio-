About me

Hey there! I'm Amanda Jeewanthi, a passionate graphic designer eager to make my mark in the creative world.

Introduction

On March 13, 2022

Welcome to My Blog,

As we embark on a new year filled with creative possibilities, it's the perfect time to explore the latest graphic design trends shaping the industry. From bold colors to innovative typography, promises to be an exciting year for graphic designers. In this blog post, I'll take a closer look at five key trends that are set to make waves in the world of design.

Let's create something extraordinary together.

From typography trends to color psychology, discover the artistry behind effective visual communication. Let's create together!

Abstract Geometry

On August 18, 2023

Abstract geometric shapes and patterns continue to dominate the design landscape in 2022. From minimalist logos to eye-catching illustrations, geometric elements add visual interest and depth to designs. Experiment with bold shapes, vibrant colors, and negative space to create captivating compositions that stand out from the crowd.

Nostalgic Aesthetics

On January 28, 2022

Nostalgia continues to influence design trends in 2022, with a resurgence of retro-inspired aesthetics. From vintage typography to throwback color palettes, designers are drawing inspiration from the past to create nostalgic yet contemporary designs. Embrace the nostalgia trend by incorporating retro elements into your work while putting a modern twist on classic designs.
