About me

Hey there! I'm Amanda Jeewanthi, a passionate graphic designer eager to make my mark in the creative world.

As a fresher in the field of graphic design, I bring a fresh perspective and a hunger to learn and grow. While I may be new to this role, my creativity knows no bounds. I thrive on the challenge of transforming ideas into captivating visual stories that leave a lasting impression.

With a keen eye for detail and a knack for innovation, I specialize in creating stunning graphics and illustrations that breathe life into every project I undertake. From crafting sleek logos that embody brand identity to designing eye-catching layouts that command attention, I pour my heart and soul into every pixel.

About me

Hi... My journey into graphic design began with a fascination for art and technology. Over time, I honed my skills through experimentation and exploration, embracing each project as an opportunity to push boundaries and unleash my creativity.

Outside of the design realm, you can often find me seeking inspiration in nature, exploring new places, or immersing myself in the vibrant world of art and culture. I believe that life is a canvas waiting to be painted, and I approach every day with a sense of curiosity and wonder.

My goal with this portfolio is to showcase not only my talent as a graphic designer but also my passion for creating meaningful and impactful visual experiences. I'm excited to embark on this journey, and I can't wait to connect with fellow creatives, collaborate on exciting projects, and make magic happen together.

Thanks for stopping by and getting to know a little bit about me. Let's create something extraordinary!

As we embark on a new year filled with creative possibilities, it's the perfect time to explore the latest graphic design trends shaping the industry. From bold colors to innovative typography, promises to be an exciting year for graphic designers. Abstract Geometry

On August 18, 2023

Abstract geometric shapes and patterns continue to dominate the design landscape in 2022. From minimalist logos to eye-catching illustrations, geometric elements add visual interest and depth to designs. Experiment with bold shapes, vibrant colors, and negative space to create captivating compositions that stand out from the crowd.

Nostalgic Aesthetics

On January 28, 2022

Nostalgia continues to influence design trends in 2022, with a resurgence of retro-inspired aesthetics. From vintage typography to throwback color palettes, designers are drawing inspiration from the past to create nostalgic yet contemporary designs. Embrace the nostalgia trend by incorporating retro elements into your work while putting a modern twist on classic designs.
